* Emacs

** How to open all files by pattern?

   #+begin_src shell
     emacs *.tex
   #+end_src

   #+begin_src shell
     emacs *.tex */*.tex
   #+end_src

   #+begin_src shell
     find . -iname \*tex -exec emacsclient {} +
   #+end_src
